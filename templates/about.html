About BookConnect

Book Connect facilitates connections between private book sellers and interested buyers who live, learn or work nearby.

A Local Alternative

BookConnect provides a convenient alternative to websites like half.com or textbook.com - no need to go to the post office to mail a book or provide credit card information online.

BookConnect also frees sellers from the tyranny of school bookstores, which buy low and sell high. YOU set the price, and avoid the middleman.

Have a book?

Complete a simple 4-item form to list your textbook on this website.

When an interested customer selects your book, BookConnect will send you an email providing you with the buyer's email address. You then contact the buyer directly to establish a meeting place to swap book and payment.

Need a book?

Search our database for the book you need. Confirm your interest and BookConnect will send an e-mail to the book seller, who will contact you directly with a meeting place to swap book and payment.
